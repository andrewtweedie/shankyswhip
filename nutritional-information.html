Nutrition Information

Is Shanky's Whip Gluten Free?

Yes, Shanky's Whip is gluten free.

Is Shanky's Whip Kosher?

No, Shanky's Whip is not Kosher certified.

Is Shanky's Whip vegetarian Friendly?

While Shanky's Whip is vegetarian, it is not registered as a vegetarian product.

Is Shanky's Whip Vegan Friendly?

While Shanky's Whip is vegan, it is not registered as a vegan product.

Is Shanky's Whip Nut Free?

Yes, Shanky's Whip is nut free.

Does Shanky's Whip have dairy?

No, there is no dairy in Shanky's Whip.

Does Shanky's whip contain any allergens?

Anyone with an allergy to caramel or any of its derivatives should not drink Shanky's Whip.

Is there any coffee in Shanky's Whip?

No, Shanky's Whip does not contain any coffee (but it tastes great when mixed with coffee!)

Does Shanky's Whip contain caffeine?

No, there is no caffeine in Shanky's Whip

What alcohol is in Shanky's Whip?

Shanky's contains neutral spirit and Irish Whiskey

How many calories are in Shanky's Whip?

Shanky's Whip contains 280 calories per 100ml

What does Shanky's taste like?

Shanky's Whip combines the delicious flavors of vanilla and caramel with Irish whiskey to create a rich "you have to try it to believe it" taste.

What should I drink Shanky's Whip with?

Shanky's Whip tastes great on its own, on the rocks, or mixed with cola. Shanky's also goes great in coffee drinks like Irish Coffee (hot or iced) and espresso martinis.

Where can I buy Shanky's Whip?

You can buy via this website or from retailers such as Amazon, Master of Malt and Whisky Exchange. We will soon be adding a store locator to our website so you can find your nearest retailer.
