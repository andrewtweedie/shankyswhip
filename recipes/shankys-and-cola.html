SHANKY'S AND COLA
Ingredients:
  • 2 oz. Shanky’s Whip
  • 6 oz. cola
  • Lemon slice

 

Instructions:
  1. Fill a glass with ice.
  2. Pour Shanky’s Whip into the glass.
  3. Pour cola in the glass.
  4. Stir until drink is thoroughly mixed.
  5. Garnish with a slice of lemon.
